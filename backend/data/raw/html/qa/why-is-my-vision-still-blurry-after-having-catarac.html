  • Why is my vision still blurry after having cataract surgery a year ago?

    NOV 12, 2020

    Question:

    Why is my vision still blurry after having cataract surgery a year ago?


    Answer:

    Persistent blurry vision after cataract surgery can be due to many different things. Some causes may relate to the surgery and others, such as macular degeneration among others, are unrelated to the surgery. See your ophthalmologist for a comprehensive exam to find the cause and the appropriate treatment.


    Answered By: Gary S Hirshfield, MD
