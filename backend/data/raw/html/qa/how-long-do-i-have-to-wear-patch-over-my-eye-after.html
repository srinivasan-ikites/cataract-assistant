  • How long do I have to wear a patch over my eye after cataract surgery?

    MAR 15, 2017

    Question:

    How long do I have to wear a patch over my eye after cataract surgery?


    Answer:

    At this time, most ophthalmologists may suggest a patch until the day after surgery, but opinions differ and times of patching are judged on a case by case basis.


    Answered By: C P Wilkinson MD

    Cataracts Surgery
