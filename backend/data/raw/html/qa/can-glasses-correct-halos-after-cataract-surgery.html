  • Can glasses correct halos after cataract surgery?

    OCT 16, 2016

    Question:

    I have had cataract surgery and I see halos around lights at night. Can a prescription for glasses correct this problem?


    Answer:

    Perhaps. But a thorough complete examination by an ophthalmologist would be necessary to answer this question appropriately.


    Answered By: C P Wilkinson MD

    Surgery Cataracts
