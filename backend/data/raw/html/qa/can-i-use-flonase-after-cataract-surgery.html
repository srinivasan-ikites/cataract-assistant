  • Can I use Flonase after cataract surgery?

    NOV 15, 2023

    Question:

    Can I use Flonase after cataract surgery?


    Answer:

    Flonase—a steroid nasal spray used for rhinitis and allergies—will likely not interfere with your healing after cataract surgery. However, to be safe, please check with your surgeon before resuming this or any other medication shortly after your procedure. Flonase and similar allergy medications can raise intraocular pressure (IOP) and increase your risk of infection.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Cataracts
