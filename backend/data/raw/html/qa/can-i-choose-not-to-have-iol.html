  • Can cataract surgery cause dizziness and headache two months later?

    FEB 17, 2025

    Question:

    I had cataract surgery in both eyes about two months ago. I have had a slight headache and dizziness over the left eye. It almost feels as if the lens is too powerful. Is this possible?


    Answer:

    Because your cataract surgeries were only two months ago, I would recommend contacting your cataract surgeon to let them know you are having these problems. Generally speaking, headache and dizziness are not typical symptoms this far out from surgery and may or may not be eye related.


    Answered By: Kurt Kelley MD

    Surgery Cataracts
