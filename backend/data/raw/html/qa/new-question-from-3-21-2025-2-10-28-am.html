  • Can I get monovision glasses after cataract surgery?

    MAR 21, 2025

    Question:

    I had cataract surgery with one eye corrected for distance and the other for near vision. After many years my distance is not so good. Can I get monovision glasses to correct this?


    Answer:

    This really depends on your correction, but if you are going to wear glasses in any way to correct your vision, I would recommend progressive lenses that will correct both eyes for all distances. If you would like monovision without glasses, contact lenses are the preferred option.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD
