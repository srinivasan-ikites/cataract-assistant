  • Is there treatment available for starbursts?

    NOV 07, 2024

    Question:

    I had a light adjustable lens (LAL) implanted in one eye 4 months ago. After YAG treatment a month later, I developed prominent starbursts around lights and it has been persistent ever since. Now, with the lens power locked in, I have good vision. But is there treatment available for the starbursts?


    Answer:

    I would have your ophthalmologists take a thorough look during a dilated eye exam.  Sometimes, simply making the YAG laser opening larger can improve these symptoms.



    Cataracts
