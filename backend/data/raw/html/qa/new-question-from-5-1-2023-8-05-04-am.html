  • Why do I feel a scratchy sensation in my eyelid after cataract surgery?

    MAY 01, 2023

    Question:

    One month after cataract surgery, why do I feel a scratchy sensation in my eyelid?


    Answer:

    It is not uncommon to feel a bit of scratchiness after cataract surgery. Your eye might feel a bit dry, and you may have sensitivity to the post-surgical drops or the sutures if they were used to seal the incision. Please discuss this and any other symptoms with your surgeon.


    Answered By: Rona Z Silkiss, MD, FACS

    Cataracts Surgery
