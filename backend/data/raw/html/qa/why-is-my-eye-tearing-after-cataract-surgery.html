  • Why is my eye tearing after cataract surgery?

    MAY 21, 2021

    Question:

    I had cataract surgery a year ago and one of my eyes has been watering continuously almost every day for the last six months. I wake up with that eye extremely crusted at times and a thick film over it that I cannot see through until I am up and blinking it for a few minutes, then it starts watering again. Can you give me an idea of what to do?


    Answer:

    This may be due to a blockage in the tear drainage duct in the eyelid.  I would see your eye surgeon to evaluate this. There is surgery to correct this, which is usually performed by an oculoplastic surgeon.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Cataracts Surgery
