  • What is the wait time between cataract surgery and blepharoplasty?

    APR 04, 2022

    Question:
     How long after cataract surgery should you wait to have upper lid blepharoplasty?

    Answer:
     Generally, cataract surgeons recommend waiting 6 weeks or so before proceeding with additional surgery. This varies depending on how the surgery went and how the eye is healing, so the wait time is best determined by your surgeon(s).

    Answered By: Rona Z Silkiss, MD, FACS

    Surgery Cataracts
