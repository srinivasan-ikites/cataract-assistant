  • Can I wear non-prescription glasses after cataract surgery?

    APR 27, 2021

    Question:

    I am considering cataract surgery with a monofocal lens for clear distance vision. I've worn eyeglasses for years, and I like the protection from brush, bugs and limbs when outdoors. Will it cause problems to continue wearing non-prescription glasses for eye protection after the surgery?


    Answer:

    There should be no problem with non-prescription protective eyewear after cataract surgery. In fact, protective eyewear (safety glasses) is strongly recommended for any activity in which there is risk of objects or debris striking the eye.


    Answered By: Gary S Hirshfield, MD

    Surgery Cataracts
