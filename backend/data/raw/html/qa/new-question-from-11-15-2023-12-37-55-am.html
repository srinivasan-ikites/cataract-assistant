  • Can a patient receive a printout copy of the ORA?

    NOV 15, 2023

    Question:

    As a patient considering ORA (Optiwave Refractive Analysis) during cataract surgery, can I obtain a copy of the monitor’s biometry data?


    Answer:

    There is no printout that I know of, although you may want to confirm this with your surgeon. The information from ORA is simply used to confirm the readings that were taken in the office.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Cataracts
