  • Will a monofocal IOL improve myopia as well as a toric IOL?

    NOV 15, 2019

    Question:

    Will a monofocal IOL improve myopia as well as a toric IOL?


    Answer:

    Both a monofocal IOL and a toric IOL can correct myopia (nearsightedness). However, if you have astigmatism, your vision without glasses will be better with a toric IOL than with a monofocal IOL. The toric lens can correct both myopia and astigmatism while the monofocal lens can only address the myopia.    


    Answered By: Gary S Hirshfield, MD

    Surgery Cataracts
