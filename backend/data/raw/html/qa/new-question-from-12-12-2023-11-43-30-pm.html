  • Is it OK if I put nexanac drops in the wrong eye?

    DEC 12, 2023

    Question:

    Is it OK if I put nexanac drops in the wrong eye?


    Answer:

    I’m assuming you were prescribed these drops to reduce pain and inflammation after cataract surgery.

    If you used these drops accidentally in the eye it was not prescribed for, it would be extremely unlikely that any problem would occur. However, it’s always best to discuss any issues involving your medication with your prescribing physician or your pharmacist.


    Answered By: Gary S Hirshfield, MD

    Cataracts
