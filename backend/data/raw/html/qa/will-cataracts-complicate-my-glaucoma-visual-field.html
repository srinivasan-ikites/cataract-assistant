  • Will cataracts complicate my glaucoma visual field test?

    JUL 28, 2017

    Question:

    Will a visual field test to check on my glaucoma be useful since I also have cataracts which affect my vision?


    Answer:

    It should be useful. Part of the software in the testing machine helps minimize the effects of the cataract.


    Answered By: James M Heltzer, MD
