  • Could I have an allergic reaction to an acrylic IOL?

    APR 24, 2018

    Question:

    I’m allergic to acrylic nail products and am considering cataract surgery. Could I also have a reaction to the acrylic IOLs placed in my eye during the surgery?


    Answer:

    I am not sure what your allergy to acrylic nail products is exactly. However, there has not been a reported allergic reaction to any of the materials in an acrylic polymer-based intraocular lens. These lenses are inert and do not leak antigens that can cause an allergy.


    Answered By: Gary S Hirshfield, MD

    Cataracts
