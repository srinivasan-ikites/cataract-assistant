  • If I have an IOL set for distance, will my near vision be worse after surgery?

    AUG 31, 2024

    Question:

    If I have a monofocal IOL set for distance, will my near vision be worse than it was before cataract surgery?


    Answer:

    Yes. The only reason you have good near vision in that eye now is you have some nearsightedness (myopia).  This will be gone after your cataract surgery, and you will require readers. 

    An alternative–if you are willing to pay out-of-pocket–is to get a more advanced IOL that focuses at both near and far distances.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Cataracts
