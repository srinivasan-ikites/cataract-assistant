  • How soon can I take Viagra after cataract surgery?

    NOV 11, 2024

    Question:

    How soon can I take Viagra after cataract surgery?


    Answer:

    Some surgeons recommend avoiding vigorous activity for a few days after cataract surgery. Please discuss this with your surgeon, who can give you a more detailed answer based on your overall health.


    Answered By: Gary S Hirshfield, MD

    Cataracts Surgery
