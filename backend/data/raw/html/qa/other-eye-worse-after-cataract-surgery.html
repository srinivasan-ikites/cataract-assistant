  • Why does my other eye seem worse after cataract surgery on the fellow eye?

    SEP 28, 2023

    Question:

    After having an IOL implanted in my "bad" eye, the "good" eye now seems more cloudy, like the old eye used to. Is that because the eye that had cataract surgery is now highlighting the fellow eye's previously unnoticed weakness?


    Answer:

    Yes, most likely both eyes had cataracts and now that the first eye has been corrected, it lets you know that the “better” eye actually had issues as well.  Plan to get the other eye done and you will be more in balance and happier with your vision.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Cataracts
