  • Do I have to stop taking Ativan before cataract surgery?

    APR 08, 2018

    Question:

    Do I have to stop taking Ativan before cataract surgery?


    Answer:

    Your doctor will usually give you an anxiety-easing or pain-relieving medication prior to the procedure that is very closely related to Ativan. So I would wait and ask on the day of surgery. Just bring the medicine with you.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Surgery Cataracts
