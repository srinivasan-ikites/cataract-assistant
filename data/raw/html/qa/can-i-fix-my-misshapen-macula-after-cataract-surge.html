  • Can I fix my misshapen macula after cataract surgery?

    APR 25, 2019

    Question:

    I have a misshapen macula resulting from an extremely difficult extraction of a very dense hyper-mature cataract. Is there a procedure to restore the macula?


    Answer:

    It’s not possible to comment on this without an in-person exam. It could be that there is macular edema or some other disease causing the shape of the macula to be other than normal. The benefit of any potential treatment to your retina will vary depending on the underlying problem. Your surgeon or retinal specialist is best equipped to discuss the condition of your retina/macula and guide any necessary treatment.


    Answered By: Abdhish R Bhavsar, MD

    Cataracts Surgery
