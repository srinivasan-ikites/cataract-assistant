  • Can I continue to wear glasses after cataract surgery?

    OCT 25, 2022

    Question:
     I’ve worn glasses for 60 years and have an upcoming cataract surgery. Is it OK replicate my current measurements, which require glasses for both distance and reading?

    Answer:
     The goal for cataract surgery is a complex decision based on the individual needs of the patient. I strongly suggest you discuss this with your surgeon. There is no reason you could not wear glasses for distance and reading or progressives or bifocals after your cataract surgery no matter what goal you and your surgeon set.

    Answered By: Gary S Hirshfield, MD
