  • What is the difference between trifocal and multifocal IOLs?

    JUL 21, 2024

    Question:

    What is the difference between trifocal and multifocal IOLs? My doctor said the multifocal will give me near, intermediate, and distance correction.


    Answer:

    Both types of IOLs (intraocular lenses) give you near, intermediate, and distance correction, but the weak point of multifocal IOLs may be intermediate or computer distance. The advantage of a trifocal is that it can have less glare and halo at night time.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Cataracts
