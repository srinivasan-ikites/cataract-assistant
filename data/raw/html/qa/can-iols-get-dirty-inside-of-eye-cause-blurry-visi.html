  • Can IOLs get dirty inside of the eye and cause blurry vision?

    NOV 26, 2016

    Question:

    I had cataract surgery12 to 13 years ago. About 4 months following the surgery, I developed posterior capsular haze (when the tissue holding your new intraocular lens in place becomes cloudy after cataract surgery) and a YAG laser was used to clear up my vision. Over time, my vision has gotten cloudy again. Can the IOLitself get "dirty?" If so, can it be cleaned? If not, what are some possible causes?


    Answer:

    The IOL being in your eye is in a sterile environment and cannot get dirty and the capsular haze rarely returns after YAG. I would go to your ophthalmologist as soon as you can and get this evaluated, as retinal and other issues can affect vision as well.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD

    Cataracts Surgery
