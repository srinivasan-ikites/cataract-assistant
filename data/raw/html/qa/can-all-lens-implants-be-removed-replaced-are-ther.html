  • Can all lens implants be removed and replaced or are there some types that cannot?

    DEC 22, 2016

    Question:

    Can all lens implants be removed and replaced or are there some types that cannot?


    Answer:

    Most, but not all, intraocular lens implants can be removed and replaced if it is medically necessary. Most insurances will only cover the cost of such an exchange in the event of a very compelling medical or optical necessity, not for convenience or small improvement in sharpness of focus. Every operation has risks and an implant exchange can be somewhat more risky if the implant is scarred into place, has had a YAG laser treatment, or some other concerns. I suggest you proceed only with the greatest of thought and consultation with your ophthalmologist.


    Answered By: Wayne F Bizer DO

    Cataracts
