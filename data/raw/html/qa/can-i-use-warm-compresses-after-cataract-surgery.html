  • Can I do warm compresses after cataract surgery?

    FEB 02, 2024

    Question:

    I had cataract surgery and now have mild blepharitis. Will warm compresses damage my new lenses?


    Answer:

    The heat will not damage your intraocular lens (IOL) as it is inside your eye and the heat is applied externally to your eyelids. But I would not do the warm compresses immediately after surgery and am assuming your operation was a month or more ago.

    This question was originally answered on Mar. 16, 2015.


    Answered By: Wayne F Bizer DO

    Cataracts Surgery
