  • Is it safe to have cataract surgery if I have an abscessed tooth?

    OCT 17, 2018

    Question:

    Is it safe to have cataract surgery if I have an abscessed tooth?


    Answer:

    As with any surgery, any active infection should be treated prior to cataract removal.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD
