  • Why is my lazy eye gone after cataract surgery?

    JUN 17, 2024

    Question:

    In 2018 I developed a lazy eye turning in. I just had cataract and astigmatism surgery. My eye is now normal and straight. What happened? I don’t need prism or glasses to see anymore.


    Answer:

    It’s hard to know for sure what lead to this improvement. But if a cataract reduces vision significantly, surgery to remove it can also improve the strabismus as it is easier for the brain to hold the images from each eye together.


    Answered By: Michael X Repka, MD, MBA
