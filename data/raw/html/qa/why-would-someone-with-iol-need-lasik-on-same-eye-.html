  • Why would someone with an IOL need LASIK on the same eye four weeks later?

    APR 24, 2016

    Question:

    Why would someone with an IOL (artificial intraocular lens placed in eye after cataract removal surgery) need LASIK on the same eye four weeks later?


    Answer:

    No one has to have LASIK after cataract surgery, but if not all of your myopia (nearsightedness), hyperopia (farsightedness) or astigmatism (irregularly shaped cornea) could be corrected with the IOL, LASIK can be used to fine tune the visual result.


    Answered By: Jeffrey Whitman, MD
