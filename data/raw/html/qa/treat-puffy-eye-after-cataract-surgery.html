  • How can I reduce puffiness under my eye after cataract surgery?

    AUG 29, 2024

    Question:

    Are there any tips to reduce puffiness under the eye after cataract surgery on a dense or mature lens? 


    Answer:

    Patients often notice puffiness under their eyes after cataract surgery. The surgery does not cause the puffiness, but enhanced vision following the surgery allows you to see the aging characteristic. Fortunately, there is cosmetic surgery called lower blepharoplasty to improve the appearance of the puffiness.


    Answered By: Rona Z Silkiss, MD, FACS
